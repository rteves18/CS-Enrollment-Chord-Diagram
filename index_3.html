UCSC Computer Science Classes Enrollment

Recent campaigns advocating more kids and teenagers to learn coding does come at a cost; enrollment in Computer Science major has skyrocketed at UCSC within the last 5 years. With recent budget cuts and the pressure to accept more students, Computer Science classes at UCSC has become more impacted than ever. Some classes even over-enroll student than its capacity while some having to turn away many students since it cannot accommodate the number of students signing up.
